Christine Addley : Caning and rushwork specialist

ABOUT ME

One of the UK's foremost experts in caning, rushwork and upholstery.

Chair seater with over 38 years experience. Commissions undertaken on antique and modern chairs in cane, natural rush, seagrass, and Danish cord. Cane work includes medallions, blind and double caning, sunrise and loom cane. The natural rush used is grown in the River Thames.
